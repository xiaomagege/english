# 英语修饰成分：so 和 such 怎么区分？

很多人学到这里会觉得 `so` 和 `such` 都像是在说：

- 这么
- 如此

结果一写句子就容易乱：

- so beautiful girl ✘
- such beautiful ✘
- so a nice day ✘

这篇文章只解决一个问题：

> **so 和 such 到底分别跟什么搭配。**

---

## 一、先记住最核心的区别

最实用的一条线是：

- `so` 主要跟 **形容词或副词**
- `such` 主要跟 **名词或带名词的结构**

例如：

- so beautiful
- so quickly
- such a beautiful day
- such good weather

你可以先把它们记成：

> **so 更靠近“性质和程度”，such 更靠近“这一类东西”。**

---

## 二、`so` 最常见的结构

### 1. `so + 形容词`

- so happy
- so kind
- so expensive

例句：

- She is so kind.
- The room is so clean.

### 2. `so + 副词`

- so quickly
- so slowly
- so carefully

例句：

- He ran so quickly.
- She answered so politely.

### 3. `so...that...`

- The bag is so heavy that I can't carry it.
- He was so tired that he went to sleep early.

这是 `so` 很高频的一个扩展结构。

---

## 三、`such` 最常见的结构

### 1. `such + a/an + 形容词 + 单数可数名词`

- such a nice boy
- such an interesting book

例句：

- He is such a polite boy.
- It is such an easy question.

### 2. `such + 形容词 + 复数名词`

- such good students
- such beautiful flowers

### 3. `such + 形容词 + 不可数名词`

- such good weather
- such useful information

这里 `such` 后面真正抓着的是名词。

---

## 四、最常见的对比

### so beautiful

这里强调的是“美”这个性质本身。

### such a beautiful girl

这里强调的是“这样一个美丽的女孩”。

所以：

- She is so beautiful. ✔
- She is such a beautiful girl. ✔

两句都对，但结构不一样，落点也不一样。

---

## 五、一些特别常见的固定搭配

除了上面的主线，你还会看到：

- so much water
- so many books
- so few people
- so little time

这里虽然 `water / books / people / time` 是名词，但前面已经有：

- much
- many
- few
- little

所以整体还是更靠近 `so` 这一边。

例如：

- We have so much work.
- She has so many friends.

---

## 六、最实用的判断方法

每次拿不准时，先看后面直接跟的是什么。

### 1. 后面直接跟形容词或副词

通常用 `so`。

- so happy
- so quickly

### 2. 后面直接跟名词结构

通常用 `such`。

- such a kind teacher
- such nice people

只要这一步看清楚，绝大多数题就能判断。

---

## 七、常见错误

### 错误 1：把 `so` 直接放到名词前

- so beautiful girl ✘
- such a beautiful girl ✔

### 错误 2：`such` 后面缺名词

- The girl is such beautiful. ✘
- The girl is so beautiful. ✔

### 错误 3：`such a/an` 漏掉冠词

- It is such interesting book. ✘
- It is such an interesting book. ✔

---

## 八、实用例句

- The box is so heavy that I can't lift it.
- She speaks so clearly.
- It is such a good idea.
- They are such friendly people.
- We had such bad weather yesterday.
- The baby is so cute.
- He made such a serious mistake.
- She has so many books at home.

---

## 九、做几个小练习

选择 `so` 或 `such`。

1. It is ___ a beautiful day.
2. She is ___ kind.
3. They are ___ helpful people.
4. He ran ___ fast.
5. We had ___ much fun.

---

## 十、参考答案

1. such
2. so
3. such
4. so
5. so

---

## 十一、一句话总结

`so` 更常跟形容词和副词，`such` 更常跟名词结构；先看后面直接接什么，是最稳的判断方法。

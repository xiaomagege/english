# 英语修饰成分：频率副词怎么用？

很多句子语法上没错，但还是显得不完整，因为缺少“平时怎样”的信息。

比如：

- I walk to school.
- She drinks tea.
- He visits his grandparents.

这时你很自然会想补一句：

- 总是
- 经常
- 通常
- 有时
- 从不

这类词就是频率副词。

---

## 一、频率副词到底在说什么

频率副词主要回答的是：

> **这个动作多常发生。**

最常见的一组是：

- always
- usually
- often
- sometimes
- seldom
- never

例如：

- I always get up early.
- She usually goes to work by bus.
- He often plays football.
- We sometimes eat out.
- They never smoke.

---

## 二、先记住大致顺序

你可以先把它们粗略记成从高到低：

> **always -> usually -> often -> sometimes -> seldom -> never**

不必一开始就把它们理解成很精确的百分比，只要先抓住语感：

- `always` 最强
- `never` 最弱
- 中间那些表示不同程度的“经常”

---

## 三、频率副词最常放在哪里

### 1. 放在普通动词前面

这是最常见的规则。

- I always get up at 6.
- She usually drinks coffee in the morning.
- They often go there on weekends.

所以：

- I go always to school by bike. ✘
- I always go to school by bike. ✔

### 2. 放在 `be` 动词后面

- He is always busy.
- She is usually late.
- They are often tired.

所以：

- He always is tired. ✘
- He is always tired. ✔

---

## 四、为什么它们常和一般现在时一起出现

因为频率副词常常在表达：

- 平时怎样
- 习惯怎样
- 经常怎样

而这些意思最自然的时态就是一般现在时。

例如：

- I usually study at night.
- She often calls her mother.
- We never eat breakfast at school.

当然，它们也不是只能跟一般现在时一起用，但初学阶段先抓住这个搭配最实用。

---

## 五、不同词的语气差别

### always

几乎每次都这样。

- He always arrives early.

### usually

大多数时候这样。

- I usually go to bed before 11.

### often

经常这样，但没有 `usually` 那么稳定。

- She often visits us.

### sometimes

有时这样。

- We sometimes cook at home.

### seldom

很少这样。

- He seldom watches TV.

### never

从不这样。

- They never drink coffee.

---

## 六、可以和时间状语一起用吗

可以，而且很常见。

- I usually get up at 6 on weekdays.
- She often goes shopping on Sundays.
- They sometimes study in the library after class.

这里：

- 频率副词告诉你“多常”
- 时间状语告诉你“什么时候”

---

## 七、常见错误

### 错误 1：位置放错

- She drinks often milk. ✘
- She often drinks milk. ✔

### 错误 2：`be` 动词句也照搬普通动词规则

- He always is late. ✘
- He is always late. ✔

### 错误 3：`never` 和 `not` 重复

- I don't never smoke. ✘
- I never smoke. ✔

### 错误 4：把习惯表达写成进行时

- I am always go to school by bus. ✘
- I always go to school by bus. ✔

---

## 八、实用例句

- I always brush my teeth before bed.
- She usually takes the bus to work.
- He often helps his classmates.
- We sometimes watch movies at home.
- They seldom go out at night.
- My father is always busy.
- The children are usually quiet in class.
- I never forget my keys.

---

## 九、做几个小练习

把括号里的频率副词放到最自然的位置。

1. I go to bed at 10. (`usually`)
2. She is late for class. (`often`)
3. They play basketball after school. (`sometimes`)
4. He drinks coffee. (`never`)
5. We are tired after work. (`always`)

---

## 十、参考答案

1. I usually go to bed at 10.
2. She is often late for class.
3. They sometimes play basketball after school.
4. He never drinks coffee.
5. We are always tired after work.

---

## 十一、一句话总结

频率副词是在补“多常发生”，普通动词前放，`be` 动词后放，是最值得先记住的基本规则。

# 英语语法里 there be 句型怎么用？

很多初学者一开始想表达“有”，会很自然地直接照中文翻：
- Have a book on the desk. ❌
- The desk has a book. △

但英语里最常见、最自然的说法通常是：

> **There is a book on the desk.**

这就是很重要的 **there be 句型**。

这篇文章只讲一个重点：**there be 句型到底什么时候用，怎么用，最容易错在哪里。**

---

## 一、there be 句型到底是什么

there be 句型最常用来表示：

> **某地有某人 / 某物。**

例如：
- There is a cat under the table.
- There are three students in the classroom.
- There was a meeting yesterday.

它的重点不是“谁拥有什么”，而是“某个地方存在什么”。

这和 have 的感觉不一样。

---

## 二、there be 的基本结构

最基本的结构是：

> **There + be + 名词 + 地点 / 时间等补充信息**

例如：
- There is a pen on the desk.
- There are many people in the park.

这里：
- There 不是真正的主语意思
- be 动词要和后面的名词保持一致

这点非常重要。

---

## 三、there is 和 there are 怎么选

### 1. there is
后面接：
- 单数名词
- 不可数名词

例如：
- There is a book on the chair.
- There is some water in the bottle.

### 2. there are
后面接：
- 复数名词

例如：
- There are two books on the chair.
- There are many students in the room.

所以：
- There is three apples on the table. ❌
- There are three apples on the table. ✅

---

## 四、there be 和 have 有什么区别

这是最容易混的地方。

### there be
强调：
- 某地有某物
- 某处存在什么

例如：
- There is a bank near my house.

### have
强调：
- 谁拥有某物

例如：
- I have a new bag.
- She has two brothers.

对比一下：

- There is a book on the desk. = 桌子上有一本书。
- I have a book. = 我有一本书。

一个是在说“某处存在”，一个是在说“拥有”。

---

## 五、there be 可以用哪些时态

### 1. 一般现在时
- There is a dog outside.
- There are many trees here.

### 2. 一般过去时
- There was a meeting yesterday.
- There were many people at the party.

### 3. 一般将来时
- There will be a test tomorrow.
- There is going to be a storm tonight.

你会发现，there be 也可以随着时间变化。

---

## 六、否定句和疑问句怎么变

### 1. 否定句
直接在 be 动词后面加 not。

- There is not a bus now.
- There are not many people here.

### 2. 一般疑问句
把 be 动词提前。

- Is there a post office near here?
- Are there any apples in the kitchen?

回答：
- Yes, there is.
- No, there isn't.
- Yes, there are.
- No, there aren't.

---

## 七、there be 里最容易忽略的点

### 1. be 动词看后面的名词
- There is a book.
- There are two books.

### 2. 后面有多个名词时，通常看离 be 最近的那个
- There is a pen and two books on the table.
- There are two books and a pen on the table.

初学阶段先抓住这个规律就够了。

### 3. 地点词常常放在后面
- There is a cat under the bed.
- There are many students in the classroom.

---

## 八、中国学习者最常见的错误

### 错误 1：把 there be 和 have 混用
- Have a book on the desk. ❌
- There is a book on the desk. ✅

### 错误 2：there is / there are 选错
- There is many students here. ❌
- There are many students here. ✅

### 错误 3：疑问句顺序错误
- There is a bank near here? ❌
- Is there a bank near here? ✅

### 错误 4：否定句结构不完整
- There not any milk. ❌
- There isn't any milk. ✅

---

## 九、实用例句

- There is a book on my desk.
- There are many people in the park.
- There is some tea in the cup.
- There was a problem yesterday.
- There were many cars on the street.
- There will be a meeting tomorrow.
- Is there a supermarket near your house?
- Are there any students in the classroom?

---

## 十、做几个小练习

### 1.
There ___ a cat under the chair.

### 2.
There ___ many books on the shelf.

### 3.
___ there a hospital near here?

### 4.
There ___ not any water in the bottle.

### 5.
There ___ a party tomorrow. (用 will)

---

## 十一、参考答案

1. is
2. are
3. Is
4. is
5. will be

---

## 十二、一句话总结

> **there be 句型用来表示“某地有某物”，there is 接单数或不可数名词，there are 接复数名词。**

---

## 十三、结尾

学会 there be 句型之后，你会发现很多“有”的表达都能一下子说得更自然。

因为很多人会说肯定句，但一到要表达“不”“没有”“不是”，句子就开始乱了。

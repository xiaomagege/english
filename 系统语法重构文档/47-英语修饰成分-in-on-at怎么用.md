# 英语修饰成分：in、on、at 怎么用？

这三个词非常高频，也非常容易混：

- in
- on
- at

因为它们都可能跟“在”有关，结果一写句子就会出错：

- I get up in 7:00. ✘
- She is at Monday. ✘
- The book is in the table. ✘

这篇文章只讲一个重点：

> **in、on、at 在时间和地点上最核心的区别。**

---

## 一、先记住一条最简单的感觉

初学阶段可以先记一个粗线条：

- `at` = 点
- `on` = 面
- `in` = 里面 / 较大范围里

这不是全部规则，但非常适合先建立直觉。

例如：

- at 7:00
- on the table
- in the room

---

## 二、表示时间时怎么区分

### 1. `at`：具体时刻

- at 7:00
- at noon
- at night
- at midnight

例句：

- I get up at 6:30.
- The class starts at 9.
- She usually studies at night.

### 2. `on`：具体某一天

- on Monday
- on May 1st
- on my birthday
- on Sunday morning

例句：

- We have a meeting on Friday.
- She was born on June 12th.
- I visited him on Sunday afternoon.

### 3. `in`：月份、年份、季节、较长时段

- in May
- in 2026
- in summer
- in the morning
- in the evening

例句：

- I was born in 2000.
- Flowers bloom in spring.
- He reads books in the evening.

---

## 三、表示地点时怎么区分

### 1. `at`：一个具体点

- at the bus stop
- at school
- at home
- at the station

例句：

- She is at home now.
- I'll meet you at the bus stop.
- He is waiting at the door.

### 2. `on`：在表面上

- on the table
- on the wall
- on the floor
- on the desk

例句：

- The book is on the table.
- There is a picture on the wall.
- My phone is on the desk.

### 3. `in`：在里面，在空间或范围中

- in the room
- in the box
- in Beijing
- in China

例句：

- She is in the kitchen.
- The keys are in the bag.
- They live in Shanghai.

---

## 四、最常见的时间对比

- at 7:00
- on Monday
- in July

- at night
- on Friday night
- in the evening

你可以先这样记：

- 点 -> `at`
- 天 -> `on`
- 月、年、季节、大范围时间 -> `in`

---

## 五、最常见的地点对比

- at the door
- on the door
- in the room

这三个意思就不一样：

- `at the door`：在门口
- `on the door`：在门上
- `in the room`：在房间里

再看：

- at school
- in the school

有时两个都可能对，但强调点不同。初学阶段先掌握最常见搭配更重要。

---

## 六、最实用的判断方法

每次拿不准时，先看你说的是：

### 1. 时间还是地点

先把这一步分清。

### 2. 是“点”“天/表面”还是“里面/范围”

- 点 -> `at`
- 天或表面 -> `on`
- 里面或大范围 -> `in`

这条主线能解决绝大多数基础用法。

---

## 七、常见错误

### 错误 1：几时几分前面用错介词

- I get up in 7:00. ✘
- I get up at 7:00. ✔

### 错误 2：星期前面用错介词

- We have class in Monday. ✘
- We have class on Monday. ✔

### 错误 3：表面和里面不分

- The cup is in the table. ✘
- The cup is on the table. ✔

### 错误 4：年份和月份前面用 `on`

- She was born on 2001. ✘
- She was born in 2001. ✔

---

## 八、实用例句

- I get up at 6.
- We have English on Tuesday.
- She was born in winter.
- The cat is on the sofa.
- My bag is in the car.
- They are at the station now.
- We met at noon.
- He stays in Beijing during the summer.

---

## 九、做几个小练习

填 `in`、`on` 或 `at`。

1. ___ 8:30
2. ___ Friday
3. ___ the wall
4. ___ the room
5. ___ 2024

---

## 十、参考答案

1. at
2. on
3. on
4. in
5. in

---

## 十一、一句话总结

`at` 更像点，`on` 更像面和具体某一天，`in` 更像里面和较大范围；先抓住这条线，后面就不会乱。

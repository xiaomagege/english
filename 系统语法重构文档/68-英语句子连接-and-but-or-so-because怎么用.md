# 英语句子连接：and、but、or、so、because 怎么用？

很多人学会写简单句之后，会马上遇到一个新问题：

- 我会说 I am tired.
- 我也会说 I want to sleep.
- 但“我累了，所以我想睡觉”怎么连起来？

这时候就要用到最基础的一批连接词：

- `and`
- `but`
- `or`
- `so`
- `because`

这篇文章只解决一个核心问题：

> **这几个最高频的连接词，到底分别在连接什么逻辑关系。**

---

## 一、先记住最核心的主线

最实用的一条线是：

- `and` 表示并列或追加
- `but` 表示转折
- `or` 表示选择
- `so` 表示结果
- `because` 表示原因

例如：

- I like tea and coffee.
- He is poor but happy.
- Do you want tea or milk?
- I was tired, so I went to bed.
- I went to bed because I was tired.

你先把这五种关系分开，句子连接就会清楚很多。

---

## 二、`and` 最常怎么用

`and` 最常表示：

- 和
- 并且
- 然后

例如：

- I like English and history.
- She opened the door and walked in.
- He is kind and funny.

它的核心感觉是：

- 把两个平行的信息接在一起

它通常不是对立，也不是原因关系。

---

## 三、`but` 最常怎么用

`but` 最常表示：

- 但是
- 可是

例如：

- I want to go, but I am too busy.
- The room is small, but it is clean.
- She studied hard, but she didn't pass the test.

它的核心感觉是：

> **前面是一个方向，后面出现反差或转折。**

---

## 四、`or` 最常怎么用

`or` 最常表示：

- 还是
- 或者

例如：

- Do you want tea or coffee?
- Is he a teacher or a doctor?
- We can go by bus or by taxi.

它的核心感觉是：

- 在几个选项里做选择

在否定句里，`or` 也很常见：

- I don't like tea or coffee.
- She doesn't have brothers or sisters.

---

## 五、`so` 最常怎么用

`so` 最常表示：

- 所以
- 结果

例如：

- I was tired, so I went to bed early.
- It was raining, so we stayed at home.
- She worked hard, so she passed the exam.

它的核心感觉是：

> **前面是情况，后面是结果。**

---

## 六、`because` 最常怎么用

`because` 最常表示：

- 因为

例如：

- I stayed at home because it was raining.
- She was happy because she passed the test.
- We were late because the traffic was bad.

它的核心感觉是：

> **后面在解释前面的原因。**

---

## 七、`so` 和 `because` 最容易混在哪里

这组特别常见。

### `because`

强调原因。

- I stayed home because it rained.

### `so`

强调结果。

- It rained, so I stayed home.

你会发现，这两句说的是同一件事，但落点不同：

- `because` 回答“为什么”
- `so` 回答“结果怎样”

还有一个特别重要的点：

- `because` 和 `so` 一般不要在同一层结构里重复连用

所以：

- Because I was tired, so I went to bed early. ✘
- I went to bed early because I was tired. ✔
- I was tired, so I went to bed early. ✔

---

## 八、常见错误

### 错误 1：`because` 和 `so` 同时乱用

- Because I was hungry, so I ate bread. ✘
- I ate bread because I was hungry. ✔
- I was hungry, so I ate bread. ✔

### 错误 2：该用 `but` 时却用 `and`

- I wanted to go out, and it was raining. △
- I wanted to go out, but it was raining. ✔

如果是明显转折，`but` 更自然。

### 错误 3：选择关系里用错连接词

- Do you want tea and coffee? △
- Do you want tea or coffee? ✔

### 错误 4：句子堆在一起但没有逻辑连接

- I was tired. I went home. I slept. △

这些句子本身没错，但如果要表达逻辑关系，就可以连起来。

---

## 九、实用例句

- I like English and math.
- He is rich but unhappy.
- Do you want rice or noodles?
- I was sick, so I didn't go to school.
- She smiled because she saw her friend.
- We can walk or take the bus.
- The room is small but clean.
- I was busy, so I didn't call you.

---

## 十、做几个小练习

选择最合适的连接词填空。

1. I was tired, ___ I went to bed early.
2. She stayed at home ___ it was raining.
3. Do you want juice ___ milk?
4. He is old, ___ he is very healthy.
5. I like apples ___ bananas.

---

## 十一、参考答案

1. so
2. because
3. or
4. but
5. and

---

## 十二、一句话总结

`and` 用来并列，`but` 用来转折，`or` 用来选择，`so` 用来接结果，`because` 用来接原因。

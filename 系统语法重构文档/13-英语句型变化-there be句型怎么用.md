# 英语语法里 there be 句型怎么用？

很多人在刚学英语时，会很快遇到这种句子：

- 桌子上有一本书。
- 房间里有两个人。
- 墙上有一张照片。

中文里这些句子都很自然，但英语不能直接按中文顺序硬翻。

英语里表达“某地有某物”时，最常见的结构是 **there be**。

这篇文章只讲一个重点：**there be 句型到底是什么意思，怎么构成，和 have 有什么区别。**

---

## 一、there be 句型到底在说什么

there be 句型最核心的意思是：

> **某地存在某人或某物。**

例如：

- There is a book on the desk.
- There are two students in the room.

这里的重点不是“谁拥有”，而是“某处存在什么”。

所以这里强调的是“存在”，不是“拥有”。

更自然地说，就是：

- 桌子上有一本书
- 房间里有两个学生

---

## 二、there is 和 there are 怎么选

最基础的规则很简单：

### 1. 后面是单数名词，用 there is

- There is a cat under the chair.
- There is an apple on the table.
- There is a problem.

### 2. 后面是复数名词，用 there are

- There are two books on the desk.
- There are many people here.
- There are some trees behind the house.

所以判断时，不是看后面的地点，而是看 **be 动词后面紧跟着的名词** 是单数还是复数。

---

## 三、there be 句型的基本结构是什么

最常见的结构是：

> **There + be + 名词 + 地点 / 时间等补充信息**

例如：

- There is a pen in my bag.
- There are five students in the classroom.
- There is a meeting this afternoon.

你可以把它看成一整套固定结构，不要拆成中文逐词翻。

---

## 四、there be 和 have 有什么区别

这是最容易混的地方。

看两句：

- There is a book on the desk.
- I have a book.

第一句的意思是：

- 桌子上有一本书

第二句的意思是：

- 我有一本书

所以：

- **there be** 强调“某地存在某物”
- **have** 强调“某人拥有某物”

不能乱换。

例如：

- The desk has a book on it. ⚠️
- There is a book on the desk. ✅

在基础阶段，表达“某地有某物”时，优先先用 there be。

---

## 五、there be 句型怎么变否定句和疑问句

### 1. 否定句

因为 there be 里面本来就有 be 动词，所以否定时很简单：

> **be 动词后面加 not**

例如：

- There is not a bus now.
- There are not any chairs in the room.

缩写形式：

- There isn't ...
- There aren't ...

### 2. 一般疑问句

把 be 动词提前：

- Is there a bank near here?
- Are there any students in the library?

回答：

- Yes, there is.
- No, there isn't.
- Yes, there are.
- No, there aren't.

---

## 六、there be 句型里最容易出错的地方

### 1. is / are 选错

- There are a book on the desk. ❌
- There is a book on the desk. ✅

- There is two books on the desk. ❌
- There are two books on the desk. ✅

### 2. 和 have 混在一起

- There has a cat in the room. ❌
- There is a cat in the room. ✅

### 3. 否定句不会写

- There not is a pen here. ❌
- There is not a pen here. ✅

### 4. 疑问句顺序乱

- There is a park near here? ❌
- Is there a park near here? ✅

---

## 七、一些非常常用的 there be 模板

### 1. There is ...

- There is a book on the table.
- There is a cat in the yard.

### 2. There are ...

- There are two windows in the room.
- There are many people outside.

### 3. Is there ...?

- Is there a post office near here?
- Is there any milk in the fridge?

### 4. Are there ...?

- Are there any students in the classroom?
- Are there enough chairs?

把这些模板用熟，表达“某地有某物”会很自然。

---

## 八、实用例句

- There is a picture on the wall.
- There are three books in my bag.
- There isn't any water in the bottle.
- There aren't many people here today.
- Is there a hospital near your home?
- Are there any apples in the kitchen?

## 九、做几个小练习

### 1.
There ___ a dog in the park.

### 2.
There ___ two pens on the desk.

### 3.
There is not some milk in the fridge.  
改成更自然的否定句。

### 4.
There are many students in the room.  
改成一般疑问句。

### 5.
我房间里有一张桌子。  
翻成英语。

---

## 十、参考答案

1. is
2. are
3. There isn't any milk in the fridge.
4. Are there many students in the room?
5. There is a desk in my room.

---

## 十一、一句话总结

> **there be 句型用来表示“某地有某人或某物”；后面是单数用 there is，后面是复数用 there are。**

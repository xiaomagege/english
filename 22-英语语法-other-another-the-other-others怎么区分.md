# 英语语法里 other、another、the other、others 怎么区分？看起来像一家人，其实差别很大

很多英语学习者看到这组词时，第一反应就是：
- 长得太像了
- 好像都和“别的”有关
- 一写句子就容易懵

比如：
- I want other book. ❌
- One is red, other is blue. ❌
- Please give me another two books. △
- Some students are inside, another are outside. ❌

这类问题非常常见。

这篇文章只讲一个重点：**other、another、the other、others 到底怎么区分。**

---

## 一、先记住最核心的主线

如果你今天只先记一个判断框架，那就是：

> **another = 另一个； other = 别的； the other = 两者中的另一个； others = 其他的人或物。**

例如：
- another book
- other students
- one ... the other ...
- some ..., others ...

先把这四条分开，已经能解决大部分问题。

---

## 二、another 怎么用

another 最常见的是：

> **另一个，再一个。**

它通常接：
- 单数可数名词

例如：
- another book
- another cup
- another day

例句：
- I need another pen.
- Can I have another cup of tea?

你可以先把 another 理解成：
- an + other
- 另一个

---

## 三、other 怎么用

other 最常见的是：

> **别的、其他的。**

它后面通常要接名词。

例如：
- other people
- other ideas
- other books

例句：
- Do you have any other questions?
- Some students stayed, and other students left.

所以 other 本身常常像一个“修饰词”，后面要有名词。

---

## 四、the other 怎么用

the other 最常见的是：

> **两个里面的另一个。**

例如：
- I have two bags. One is black, and the other is brown.
- She has two sons. One lives in Beijing, and the other lives in Shanghai.

这里的重点是：
- 已经明确是两者
- 剩下的那一个就是 the other

---

## 五、others 怎么用

others 最常见的是：

> **其他的人 / 其他的东西。**

它后面通常不再接名词，因为它自己已经代替了名词。

例如：
- Some students are reading, and others are writing.
- I like these shoes. Others are too expensive.

所以：
- other students ✅
- others ✅
- others students ❌

---

## 六、几组最典型的对比

### another vs other
- another book = 另一本书
- other books = 其他书

### the other vs others
- the other = 两个中的另一个
- others = 其他一些

例如：
- I have two dogs. One is big, and the other is small.
- Some dogs are quiet, but others are noisy.

这组对比非常重要。

---

## 七、最实用的判断方法

你先问自己三个问题：

### 1. 你说的是“另一个单独的”吗？
- 是 → another

### 2. 你说的是“别的 + 名词”吗？
- 是 → other

### 3. 你说的是“两者剩下的那一个”还是“其他一些”？
- 两者剩下的那一个 → the other
- 其他一些 → others

这样拆开就不容易乱。

---

## 八、中国学习者最常见的错误

### 错误 1：other 后面不接名词
- I need other. ❌
- I need another one. ✅
- I need other books. ✅

### 错误 2：others 后面还接名词
- Others people disagree. ❌
- Other people disagree. ✅

### 错误 3：两者场景不用 the other
- I have two sisters. One is a teacher, another is a doctor. △
- I have two sisters. One is a teacher, the other is a doctor. ✅

### 错误 4：把 another 和 other 混用
- Do you have another questions? ❌
- Do you have any other questions? ✅

---

## 九、实用例句

- I need another chance.
- Some people agree, but others disagree.
- Do you have any other ideas?
- One child was crying, and the other was sleeping.
- She bought another dress yesterday.
- Other students finished earlier.
- This bag is mine; the other one is hers.
- Some books are easy, and others are difficult.

---

## 十、做几个小练习

选择最合适的词填空。

### 1.
I need ___ pen.

### 2.
Do you have any ___ questions?

### 3.
One is mine, and ___ is yours.

### 4.
Some students are singing, and ___ are dancing.

### 5.
___ people may think differently.

---

## 十一、参考答案

1. another
2. other
3. the other
4. others
5. Other

---

## 十二、一句话总结

> **another 表示另一个，other 表示别的并常接名词，the other 表示两个中的另一个，others 表示其他一些人或物。**

---

## 十三、结尾

只要你不再把这几个词都模糊地记成“其他”，它们其实没有想象中那么乱。

因为这组词虽然很早就学过，但真到写句子时，单复数和远近感还是很容易混。

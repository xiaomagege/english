# 英语时态语态：一般将来时怎么用？

当你想说下面这些话的时候：

- 我明天要去上学
- 她下周会来看我
- 我们今晚要做作业

你表达的都不是现在，也不是过去，而是**将来**。

这时候你就要用到 **一般将来时**。

但很多初学者一开始容易混乱：

- I will goes there. ❌
- She is go to visit me tomorrow. ❌
- We going to play tonight. ❌

这篇文章只讲一个重点：**一般将来时到底怎么用，最常见的两种表达是什么。**

---

## 一、什么是一般将来时

一般将来时用来表示：

1. **将来会发生的事**
2. **将来的计划或打算**
3. **临时决定或预测**

你可以先记一句最实用的话：

> **一般将来时 = 还没发生，但将来会发生的事。**

例如：

- I will call you tonight.
- She is going to study abroad.
- It will rain tomorrow.

---

## 二、一般将来时最常见的两种表达

英语里最常见的是这两种：

### 1. will + 动词原形

例如：

- I will help you.
- He will come later.
- They will be busy tomorrow.

### 2. be going to + 动词原形

例如：

- I am going to buy a new bag.
- She is going to visit her grandmother.
- We are going to watch a movie.

基础阶段你可以先把它们都理解成“表示将来”的常用表达。

更细的区别，后面再单独讲。

---

## 三、will 怎么用

### 1. 表示临时决定

例如你刚刚想到一件事：

- The phone is ringing. I will answer it.
- I'm thirsty. I will get some water.

这里不是早就计划好的，而是说话时当场决定。

### 2. 表示判断、预测

- I think it will rain tomorrow.
- You will like this book.

### 3. 表示承诺、意愿

- I will help you.
- I will never forget this.

结构非常简单：

> **will + 动词原形**

所以：

- He will goes there. ❌
- He will go there. ✅

---

## 四、be going to 怎么用

### 1. 表示事先计划好的事

- I am going to study tonight.
- We are going to travel next month.
- She is going to see a doctor tomorrow.

### 2. 表示根据迹象判断将要发生的事

- Look at those clouds. It is going to rain.
- Be careful! You are going to fall.

结构是：

> **am / is / are + going to + 动词原形**

所以：

- She is go to visit me tomorrow. ❌
- She is going to visit me tomorrow. ✅

- We going to play tonight. ❌
- We are going to play tonight. ✅

---

## 五、一般将来时常和哪些词一起出现

这些时间词很常见：

- tomorrow
- tonight
- next week
- next year
- soon
- in the future

例如：

- I will see you tomorrow.
- She is going to leave next week.
- We will finish it soon.

看到这些词时，通常要想到将来时。

---

## 六、否定句和疑问句怎么变

### 1. will 的否定句

- I will not go.
- She will not come.

缩写：

- won't

例如：

- He won't be late.

### 2. will 的一般疑问句

把 will 提前。

- Will you help me?
- Will they come tomorrow?

### 3. be going to 的否定句

在 be 动词后加 not。

- I am not going to go out.
- She is not going to stay here.

### 4. be going to 的一般疑问句

把 be 动词提前。

- Are you going to study tonight?
- Is he going to play football?

---

## 七、中国学习者最常见的错误

### 错误 1：will 后面不小心加了变化后的动词

- She will goes there. ❌
- She will go there. ✅

### 错误 2：be going to 结构不完整

- I going to sleep early. ❌
- I am going to sleep early. ✅

### 错误 3：be going to 后面不是动词原形

- He is going to studies abroad. ❌
- He is going to study abroad. ✅

### 错误 4：把两个结构和现在进行时混在一起

- We are going play tonight. ❌
- We are going to play tonight. ✅

---

## 八、实用例句

- I will call you after dinner.
- She will be happy to see you.
- We will visit the museum tomorrow.
- I am going to clean my room tonight.
- He is going to learn Chinese next year.
- Are you going to watch the game?
- It will be cold tomorrow.
- Look out! You are going to drop it.

---

## 九、做几个小练习

### 1.
I ___ call you tomorrow.

### 2.
She is going to ___ (visit) her aunt next week.

### 3.
They ___ not come tonight. (用 will)

### 4.
___ you going to study this evening?

### 5.
He will ___ (help) us.

---

## 十、参考答案

1. will
2. visit
3. will
4. Are
5. help

---

## 十一、一句话总结

> **一般将来时表示将来会发生的事，最常见的表达是 will + 动词原形 和 be going to + 动词原形。**

---

## 十二、结尾

学会一般将来时之后，你就能把过去、现在、将来这三条最基础的时间线都表达出来。

接下来最适合继续学的，就是：**现在完成时怎么用。**
